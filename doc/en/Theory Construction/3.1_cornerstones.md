#### 3.1 Empirical Cornerstones and Meta-Philosophical Declaration

##### 3.1.1 The Five Empirical Cornerstones

We take the following five universally verified empirical facts of modern physics as the **sole inputs** for constructing Generalized Existence Theory:

1. **Heisenberg uncertainty principle (energy-time constraint of quantum fluctuations)**  
   Experimentally confirmed: persistent random quantum fluctuations exist in vacuum. The energy scale ΔE of an individual fluctuation and its duration Δt are rigidly constrained:  
   ΔE · Δt ≥ ℏ/2  
   This relation is the **insurmountable rule** enabling the existence of quantum fluctuation phenomena.

2. **Power-law statistics in the extremely large-number limit (energy-level distribution of fluctuations)**  
   In regimes involving an extremely large number of quantum fluctuations, the probability distribution of fluctuation energy scales ΔE stably obeys a power law:  
   P(ΔE) ∝ ΔE^(-α), α > 1  
   This power-law distribution is the **universal statistical mode** characterized by rare high-energy events dominating low-energy ones.

3. **The three rigid boundaries of the laws of thermodynamics**  
   - **First Law (energy conservation)**: Energy can be transformed between forms, but the total quantity is conserved. This is the absolute accounting of cosmic evolution.  
   - **Second Law (entropy increase)**: The entropy of an isolated system never decreases; processes possess irreversible directionality. This is the directional boundary of cosmic evolution.  
   - **Third Law (absolute zero unattainable)**: No finite sequence of operations can reduce a system to absolute zero temperature. This reveals the fundamental constraint of existence — a fully “frozen” morphology is physically impossible.

4. **Einstein’s mass-energy equivalence relation**  
   E = m c² holds exactly in all relevant processes. It reveals that **“energy” and “mass” are not independent ontologies, but two morphological expressions of the same underlying reality**. The speed of light c is the **universal conversion constant** between them.

5. **Rule-dependence of all structured existence**  
   Every observed stable, non-trivial structure — from elementary particles to organisms and societies — depends for its existence, maintenance, and interactions on a set of **intrinsic, hierarchical, describable rules that are objective and independent of consciousness** (physical laws, genetic code, social contracts, etc.). Execution and maintenance of these rules require continuous energy input. **The rules themselves exist objectively**.

##### 3.1.2 Core Meta-Philosophical Declaration: The Ultimate Resolution of the “Phenomenon vs. Law” Dilemma

Modern physics confronts us with a fundamental ontological puzzle: persistent, rigidly constrained quantum fluctuations occur in an almost “nothing” vacuum — so which comes first, the **energy-borrowing phenomena** or the **Heisenberg constraint and power-law distribution laws**?

1. **Phenomena precede laws** → physical laws become mere after-the-fact statistics; the world’s foundation is lawless. This path leads to chaos and agnosticism.  
2. **Laws precede phenomena** → laws become transcendent metaphysical entities detached from physical process itself. This path abandons naturalism.  
3. **Rule execution is phenomenon; the two are one and the same** → this is the only logically self-consistent path.

1. Is Δt in the Heisenberg uncertainty principle a mathematical point (i.e., zero) or a process?

3. The Earth-Moon system is a system; why cannot true vacuum be a system? It must be — because true vacuum satisfies every defining criterion of a system!
